Privacy Policy for StageMon

Last updated: June 9, 2026

StageMon is developed by Via Nubium.

No data collection

StageMon does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Network usage

StageMon communicates exclusively with audio devices (such as the Behringer XR18) on your local network via OSC protocol. No data is sent to external servers or the internet.

Local storage

StageMon saves mixer snapshots and screen layout preferences locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is used solely to restore your settings between sessions.
